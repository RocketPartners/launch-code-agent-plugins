title: Daily Update Examples
---

> How do I send a daily update?
> Just say "send daily update" or "/daily-update" and it will auto-detect your work from git, GitHub PRs, and Obsidian notes, then send a formatted message to the Slack channel you choose.

> What does the update look like?
> *Daily Update — launch-code — 2026-02-26*
>
> *Ready for Review*
> • Fix: Slug validation on agents/new and tables/new — https://github.com/org/repo/pull/110
>
> *In Progress*
> • Investigating chat analytics timeout issue
>
> *Merged Today*
> • Perf: Cache prompt_count (#90)

> Can I customize which channel it goes to?
> Yes — it will ask you which channel before sending. You can also pass it as an argument: `/daily-update rp-ph-ai-tiger-team`
